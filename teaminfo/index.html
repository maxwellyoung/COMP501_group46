Code of conduct: As a project team, we will:

  • Prioritize and organize the tasks and allocate them to each member.
  • Solve any obstacles or interpersonal problems while collaborating.
  • Ensure to achieve the final goal.

Participation: We will:

  • Provide the opportunity to contribute to the project for all team members.
  • Ensure team members are attending the meetings.
  • Contribute and manage workload fairly.

Communication: We will:

  • Communicate via Discord and centralize the work on Google Drive and Github.
  • Be responsive and keep discussing the projects.
  • Helpful and present in all meetings.

Problem solving: We will:

  • Ask assistance from TA if required.
  • Collaborate on any obstacles we encounter.
  • Support each team member to solve problems.

Meeting guidelines: We will:

  • Take a meeting minutes in every team meeting.
  • Stay focused and active on the meetings.
  • Ensure to have productive meetings.
