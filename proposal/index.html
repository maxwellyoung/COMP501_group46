Brief description of our topic, its relevance and importance and why you have chosen it
Topic Description Description and why it is important
The Safety Concern of Autonomous Cars The advancement of AI accelerated the deployment of autonomous cars. However, numerous issues arose when adopting this technology into our life. One of the most concerning issues is the safety of autonomous cars. Despite the fact that big tech companies are investing into developing autonomous cars, fatalities due to autonomous cars still occurred and killed pedestrians. They are mainly caused by the failure to sense the objects by its machine learning program or poor system deployment coded by humans (Baecker, 2019). Hence, we know it is not easy to deploy autonomous cars that are perfectly safe.

We believe autonomous cars require careful consideration of traffic controls to improve safety concerns. If autonomous cars are successful in carrying out safe operations, the impact on society will be immeasurable. Therefore, our team will investigate this problem and propose how our society should adopt autonomous cars safely.
