Topic Description Description and why it is important
AI Security Risks in Open-Source Software Recently, AI-related software has also been open-sourced, with major companies like Google and Microsoft releasing critical AI tools such as TensorFlow to the public. This openness fosters a collaborative environment where developers can build upon existing technologies without starting from scratch, accelerating technological advancements.

Safety and Privacy: Protecting sensitive data and ensuring user privacy is paramount in the digital age. Vulnerabilities in OSS can lead to data breaches and privacy violations. Innovation and Collaboration: A secure open-source environment encourages continued innovation and collaboration within the global tech community, fostering growth and development. Regulatory Compliance: Adhering to cybersecurity best practices helps organizations comply with data protection regulations, avoiding legal issues and penalties. Sustainable Development: Ensuring the security and reliability of OSS supports the long-term sustainability of software projects, benefiting both developers and users.

Our team will explore the cybersecurity and AI security risks associated with open-source software, examining the socio-economic implications, potential vulnerabilities, and strategies for mitigation. By understanding and addressing these challenges, we aim to contribute to a safer and more secure open-source community, ultimately supporting the continued growth and adoption of innovative technologies. for this
